BLink

Making shared experiences with friends simpler

More about our project

Pain point

Remember that time when you and your friends went on an amazing trip, only to get sour at the end when everyone had to wrestle over who pays what? BLink aims to solve this by enabling group registration, tracing the group's transactions and automating payments between the group members. It further analyses purchasing patterns and provides recommendations for destinatios where members can redeem cash reward points. This in turn enables the bank to optimize each member's reward program for increased savings

Value Proposition

For Customers: BLink helps you set up groups to calculate expenses, initialise and execute payment agreements. All this is made possible through Citi’s, and in the future, other banks’ Credit Card and Money Transfer APIs. On top of that, BLink proposes shared rewards points so that at least one of you in your group would make you go use those rewards points you never use.

For CitiBank: BLink does more than looking after the consumers. She also takes care of other people in the Credit Card ecosystem, Issuing Banks and their Retail partners.
